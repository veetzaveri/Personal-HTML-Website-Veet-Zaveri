My favorite restaurant is Pepe’s Mexican Restaurant for several reasons, and my favorite menu item is Garnachas. Garnachas are chips layered with beans, ranchera sauce, and melted cheddar. It also is served with guacamole and sour topping on the side. I like this restaurant because it seems that I never get tired of eating this same menu item even after many years. Also, this restaurant is not expensive at all while having both dine in and carry out. My typical meal can cost about $8 or so which is not that expensive for a restaurant that isn’t fast food.
